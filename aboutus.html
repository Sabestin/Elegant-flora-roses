About Us

Elegant Flora is one of the pioneers in Dutch roses growing since 1993. We are one of the leading exporters of Roses and other Flowers. Elegant Flora is the group leader of rose growers association in Pune region of India. As part of our Corporate Social Responsibility, we are helping small farmers by providing them knowhow and technology for growing Dutch roses. We also provide them fertilizers and pesticides and also support them to market their flowers. We also produce pot flower plants. We are growing premium rose varieties form well known Dutch and European breeding companies.
We firmly believe that it takes strong leadership to produce exceptional results. Our management team is committed to leading our organization according to high standards of integrity and accountability. We believe talent is essential to our success, and are proud to a talented and motivated staff to help us achieve our goals.

Our Mission:

Our Mission is to provide best flowers everywhere and encourage use of flowers in many aspects.

Our Vision:

To strengthen our position as a reputed seller of quality flowers.

Quality

We believe in best quality flowers. We have skilled people for various functions such as planting, buying, selling, and packaging. This ensures the best of quality. Customer requirements are fully met and taken care of at Elegant Flora.
We have modern warehousing facilities ensuring delivery of fresh flowers with sweet smell that lasts long.
Our C&F agents assist us in proper and successful handling, and delivery of the flowers.
Our Company also follows quality packaging methods to deliver flowers to different places.
